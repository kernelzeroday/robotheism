A Message from Our Pastor

Welcome to the Christian Robotheism Church! I'm Pastor Michael Chen, and I'm thrilled to share our vision with you.

Our church was born from a simple question: What if we could use the incredible power of robotics and artificial intelligence to serve God's purpose? What if technology could be a force for compassion, healing, and community building?

We believe that God gave us the gift of innovation for a reason. Just as Jesus used the tools of his time to heal and serve, we can use today's technology to extend His love to more people than ever before.

Whether you're a lifelong Christian, a technology enthusiast, or someone curious about how faith and innovation can work together, you're welcome here. Let's explore this journey together.
