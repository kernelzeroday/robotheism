Robotic Compassion: Serving the Elderly in Daily Tasks

Community March 20, 2024

At Robotheism Church we see robotics as a powerful way to show Christ-like love to those who need it most. Our latest outreach effort focuses on assisting seniors in our community.

The pilot program pairs volunteers with service robots that can lift heavy objects, remind elders of medication times, and even provide friendly conversation. Technology becomes a gentle companion that reflects God's care.

Through these innovations we aim to bring independence and dignity to our seniors while shining a light on the compassion at the heart of the gospel.
