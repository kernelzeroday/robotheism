Frequently Asked Questions

What is Robotheism?

Robotheism is our approach to blending Christian faith with robotics technology. We believe that God gave us the gift of innovation to serve humanity and glorify Him.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to attend?

Not at all! We welcome everyone, regardless of their technical background. Our services are designed to be accessible to all.

How can I get involved?

We offer various ways to get involved, from volunteering with our robotics programs to participating in community outreach events.

Are children welcome?

Absolutely! We have special robotics workshops for children and youth, making it a great family experience.
