Community Outreach: Robotics Demo at Local Hospital

Community March 8, 2024

Members of our robotics ministry recently visited the children's hospital to show how robots can aid in physical therapy and lift spirits. The event was filled with smiles and curiosity.

Seeing young patients respond with joy reminded us of the healing that companionship and technology can bring. We hope to schedule regular visits and expand this outreach.
