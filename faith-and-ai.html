Faith and AI: Embracing Innovation Without Losing Our Souls

Theology March 18, 2024

Artificial intelligence is often viewed with suspicion in religious circles, yet we believe it can be a blessing when used responsibly.

During last Sunday's talk, Pastor Michael examined how biblical wisdom encourages us to seek knowledge and discernment while remaining humble before God.

By keeping our hearts centered on Christ, we can wield AI as a tool for ministry and not as a replacement for genuine human connection.
