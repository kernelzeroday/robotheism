Technology as Divine Tool: Building Robots with Purpose

Sermon March 15, 2024

Pastor Michael teaches that technology can reflect God's creativity when we build with compassion and care. Robots are more than machines—they are instruments that can multiply our ministry.

This sermon explores biblical stories of innovation and connects them with modern robotics research. We are called to use every tool at our disposal to spread love and hope.

By dedicating our work to the Lord, we can develop robots that serve people in need and demonstrate that faith and science walk hand in hand.
